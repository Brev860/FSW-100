2020 Championship Bound Celtics

After cleaning house the last two years, the Celitcs have finally realized the potential of their younger players. By following a no super star mentality known only to the New England Patriots they have centered their focus around Jayson, Kemba, and Jaylen. Cutting dead weight like Kyrie Irving, and Issaih Thomas whoes superstar mentality hurt the team help empower the future franchise players. Although fans are feeling the loss of senior players such as Horford. After back to back appearances in the playoffs this may be the year Bostom claims another championship.
