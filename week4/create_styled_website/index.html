The Genesis: In The Begining

In the early 70's in New York City a movement was being born. Due to lack of canvas, acrylic paints, brush, etc... the youths of the city bgain using spray paint in innovative ways. This was the begining of Graffiti. This artform told a story of the climate of the inner city from the viewpoint of those directly affected by it. It also was the only way for them to to say I was here"..."I existed".
